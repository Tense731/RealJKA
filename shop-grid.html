Our Affiliations

At JKASoccerSolutions, we take pride in our strategic partnerships with leading organizations, brands, and service providers in the soccer industry. These affiliations allow us to offer high-quality products, services, and innovative solutions to our clients, coaches, and athletes. By aligning with trusted partners, we can continue to enhance the soccer experience and ensure that our customers receive the best in training, equipment, and performance.

Our Esteemed Partners We are proud to collaborate with a wide range of influential organizations, both locally and internationally, who share our commitment to the growth and development of soccer. These partnerships help us bring advanced technologies, expert coaching, and the latest in soccer innovations to all levels of the game.

1. Equipment & Gear Suppliers

We’ve partnered with top-tier soccer gear manufacturers and retailers to ensure that our athletes have access to the best equipment on the market. From cleats to goalkeeper gloves, training kits to performance-enhancing wearables, we offer a variety of premium products tailored to every player's needs. Our equipment partnerships include: Adidas: As one of the leading sports brands in the world, our collaboration with Adidas brings cutting-edge soccer gear, apparel, and footwear to our clients. From boots designed for speed and control to custom team kits, Adidas helps us enhance the soccer experience for players of all ages. Nike: Known for its innovative designs and performance-enhancing technology, Nike’s partnership with JKASoccerSolutions ensures that our players receive the highest-quality gear for training and match play. Puma: With a focus on performance and style, Puma’s collaboration with us allows us to provide our athletes with lightweight, durable soccer gear that helps them perform at their best. Under Armour: We collaborate with Under Armour to provide training apparel and gear that is engineered to support high-performance athletes, including moisture-wicking fabrics and flexible, durable materials for intense training sessions.

2. Coaching & Training Partners

We believe in the importance of continuous learning and development for coaches and players alike. Our coaching and training partners offer professional resources, certifications, and coaching programs that help elevate the standard of soccer in every community we serve. U.S. Soccer Federation: As a trusted partner of U.S. Soccer, we are aligned with the national body that governs soccer in the United States. This affiliation ensures that our coaching programs are aligned with the latest training methods, strategies, and player development standards. NSCAA (National Soccer Coaches Association of America): Through our partnership with NSCAA, we offer world-class coaching certifications and educational resources to our coaches. This collaboration ensures our coaching staff remains at the forefront of soccer education. The Coaching Manual: A key partner in providing digital resources and tools for soccer coaches, The Coaching Manual offers training videos, session plans, and tactical advice to help coaches enhance their methods and improve player development.

3. Technology & Analytics Partners

In today’s fast-evolving sports world, data and technology play an essential role in improving player performance. Our technology partners provide the latest tools to analyze player performance, track progress, and enhance tactical development. StatsBomb: A leader in soccer analytics, StatsBomb offers us advanced data solutions for in-depth performance analysis. This partnership allows us to offer players and coaches a detailed understanding of game dynamics and individual performance metrics. Hudl: Through Hudl’s video analysis platform, we provide teams and athletes with detailed performance reviews that include match breakdowns, highlight reels, and tactical insights. This partnership ensures players get the feedback they need to improve both individually and as a team. Catapult Sports: Catapult’s performance monitoring devices are integrated into our training programs, offering insights into players' physical data such as speed, distance, and fatigue. With this technology, we can tailor training loads to optimize player performance and recovery.

4. Youth Development Programs

Our mission is to nurture young talent, and our affiliations with youth soccer development programs allow us to build solid foundations for future generations of athletes. AYSO (American Youth Soccer Organization): AYSO is a pivotal partner in our efforts to support youth development. Through this affiliation, we collaborate on grassroots initiatives, tournaments, and coaching programs to introduce young players to the sport and provide them with opportunities for growth. US Youth Soccer: As part of US Youth Soccer, we participate in tournaments, development programs, and initiatives that help discover and nurture young talent. This affiliation connects us with thousands of young athletes, ensuring we provide the best possible development environment. The Elite Player Development Network: Through this network, we have access to top-level coaching resources and professional exposure for talented youth players. The Elite Player Development Network enables us to provide our athletes with unique opportunities to showcase their skills at a national level.

Global Affiliations

As we expand internationally, our global partnerships reflect our commitment to bringing the best of soccer to players across the world. Through our international affiliates, we can access soccer innovations, training methods, and opportunities for athletes from different backgrounds. FIFA (Fédération Internationale de Football Association): Our connection with FIFA allows us to stay aligned with global soccer standards and regulations. This partnership ensures that we provide players, coaches, and fans with up-to-date information and standards in the soccer world. La Liga: As one of the top soccer leagues globally, La Liga’s collaboration with JKASoccerSolutions provides us with access to professional-level coaching, player development, and soccer culture. We work together to promote the sport and help aspiring players from all over the world develop their skills. FC Barcelona Academy: Our partnership with FC Barcelona’s Academy gives our players exclusive access to world-class training methodologies and elite coaching techniques, all inspired by the success and philosophies of one of the most successful football clubs globally.

Community Engagement and Charity Partnerships We believe in the power of soccer to inspire change. Through our partnerships with non-profit organizations and community initiatives, we aim to give back and support underprivileged communities by offering access to soccer programs, equipment, and coaching. Kicks for Kids: This charity initiative works to provide soccer gear, coaching, and opportunities to children in underserved communities. As part of this partnership, JKASoccerSolutions regularly donates equipment and organizes fundraising events to help provide soccer opportunities to youth who otherwise wouldn’t have access. Soccer Without Borders: This international organization uses soccer as a tool for social change, providing opportunities for refugee and immigrant youth to thrive. Our partnership focuses on supporting their mission through donations, training sessions, and volunteer coaching.

In Conclusion

At JKASoccerSolutions, we are proud of the relationships we have cultivated with our partners, and we are committed to continuously improving the soccer experience for players and coaches at all levels. By collaborating with these esteemed organizations, we can ensure our clients have access to the best training tools, equipment, and opportunities, helping them achieve success both on and off the field. We look forward to growing our network and continuing to provide excellence in soccer solutions for years to come.

At JKA SOCCER SOLUTIONS, we strive to provide an environment which cultivates individual growth and self-motivated learning to help you become the best version of yourself. Let our team of passionate, ambitious, and knowledgeable coaches inspire you to unlock your full potential.
