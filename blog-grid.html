    EXPERICEND TEAM

    Established in 1997, Soccer Solutions has over 25 years experience of hosting small sided football games at various venues across Dorset and Hampshire. We pride ourselves as being the friendliest and easiest gateway to play 6-a-side football on the South Coast. Run by Bob Kitley, our competitive but friendly leagues have gained a reputation for being some of the best in Bournemouth, Poole and beyond! We’re active in the community, and we support the Steve Bernard Foundation, a charity which was set up in memory of the affectionately named local footballer “Stevie B”, following his tragic passing in a car accident in November 2005.
